My favourites!

Here is a collection of my favourite things! Click to learn more:

Food

Honestly, there are too many delicious things out there for me to decide on just one, but I will say that the massaman curry from my favourite local restaurant is one of the nicest things I have ever had. I'm also a huge fan of Japanese dishes like sushi, teriyaki and katsu curry, though Indian and Italian food is also amazing.

Sport

Without a doubt, my absolute favourite sport is archery. I joined my college's archery club when I started in first year, and it didn't take long for me to fall in love with it. I have my own bow and arrows now, and I have a range set up at home to practise. I also enjoy GAA, rugby and darts, but more as a spectator than a player.

Music

I listen to a lot of different genres, so it is really difficult for me to pick just one! My favourite artist is probably Hozier, he has an amazing voice and his lyrics are so poetic. I also like emo/pop-punk music (think Fall Out Boy or My Chemical Romance, as well as more modern artists like half.alive and IDKHOW), and comedy music (NSP and Danny Gonzales).

Movie

To tell the truth, I don't watch very many movies, but if I had to pick one it would be the 2000 film Snatch, directed by Guy Richie. The film is incredibly funny, and the way all of the seperate story lines cross and link together is so cool to see. I have also watched all of the MCU films multiple times, so it would be unfair to leave them off this list.

TV series

Much like movies, I do not watch that much TV, but I do have a few favourites. Avatar: The Last Airbender and it's sequel Legend of Korra are amazing shows that I watched as a child and still love now. I also enjoyed the anime Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood, and more recently I have also watched Erased and BNA, and I really recommend them.

Video Game

I really enjoy video games, Nintendo games in particular. My favourite series is the Legend of Zelda, and my favourite Zelda game is Breath of the Wild. The storyline is super intricate, and the graphics are absolutely beautiful. I also enjoy Animal Crossing, and have recently started playing some Assassin's Creed games, though there are not many available for the Nintendo Switch.

Pastime

One of my favourite things to do is play boardgames. During the college semester, my friends and I meet weekly to play together. I love all kinds of boardgames, from the classics like Monopoly and Cluedo, to more obscure games like Betrayal at House on the Hill and Nothing Personal. This also extends to card games of all kinds, with my favourite at the minute being the traditional Japanese game Hanafuda.
